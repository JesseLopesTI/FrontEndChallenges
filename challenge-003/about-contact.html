Ludwig Favre, Born in France in 1976, in north of Paris.

Ludwig Favre is a Photographer specializing in major cities and landscapes of america, raised in Paris, currently living in Paris. He has created visuals on a variety of media platforms from advertising campaigns to magazine editorials, books, galleries over the world.
